Frequently Asked Questions

Maswali Yanayoulizwa Mara kwa Mara

Who can join the program?

Nani anaweza kujiunga na mpango?

Youth aged 18-35 from East Africa, with a focus on rural and female participants.

Vijana wenye umri wa miaka 18-35 kutoka Afrika Mashariki, wakizingatia washiriki wa vijijini na wanawake.

Is the training free?

Je, mafunzo ni bure?

Yes, fully funded for qualified applicants, including devices for low-access areas.

Ndiyo, yanafadhiliwa kabisa kwa waombaji waliostahili, ikijumuisha vifaa kwa maeneo yenye upatikanaji wa chini.

How long are the courses?

Kozi zina muda gani?

3-6 months, with flexible online schedules for working youth.

Miezi 3-6, na ratiba zinazobadilika za mtandaoni kwa vijana wanaofanya kazi.

What jobs can I get after?

Ninaweza kupata kazi gani baada ya hapo?

Remote gigs in AI, coding, or local startups in agri-tech and health.

Kazi za mbali katika AI, kuandika kodu, au kampuni zinazoanza za ndani katika teknolojia ya kilimo na afya.

How do I apply?

Ninaombaje?

Use the form on the Training page or email info@techbridgeea.org.

Tumia fomu kwenye ukurasa wa Mafunzo au tuma barua pepe kwa info@techbridgeea.org.

Are there certificates?

Je, kuna vyeti?

Yes, recognized by partners like Cisco, upon completion.

Ndiyo, vinavyotambuliwa na washirika kama Cisco, baada ya kumaliza.
