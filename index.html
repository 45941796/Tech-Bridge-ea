Our Mission

Dhamira Yetu

Empowering East African youth with digital skills for global opportunities, starting in rural Kenya and scaling to Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda.

Kuwapa vijana wa Afrika Mashariki ujuzi wa kidijitali kwa fursa za kimataifa, kuanzia vijijini Kenya hadi Tanzania, Uganda, na Rwanda.

Our Vision

Maono Yetu

To create a tech-savvy generation that drives innovation, reduces unemployment, and builds sustainable communities across East Africa by 2030.

Kuunda kizazi chenye ujuzi wa teknolojia kinachoongoza uvumbuzi, kupunguza ukosefu wa ajira, na kujenga jamii endelevu katika Afrika Mashariki ifikapo 2030.
